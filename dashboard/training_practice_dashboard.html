Method: composite training gain = mean(Δknowledge, Δskill, Δattitude), EL−BL. Practice delta = mean(post-training visit composites) − pre-training visit composite. Quadrant thresholds: gain ≥ 1.0 / 5 = "high gain", delta ≥ 0.5 / 5 = "meaningful improvement" — fixed bars, not cohort-relative, so they stay stable across training cycles. Teachers missing a baseline, follow-up, full assessment, or any observation are excluded from quadrant scoring and shown in Data Health instead of being silently dropped or averaged in.
